1. What is slope?

Slope is represented by model coefficients. It indicates how much the target variable changes when a feature increases by one unit.

2. What is intercept?

Intercept is the predicted value when all feature values are zero.

3. What do they mean?

The slope shows the impact of each feature on marks. The intercept is the baseline prediction.

1. What is MAE?

Mean Absolute Error measures the average prediction error.

2. What is R² Score?

R² measures how much variation in the target variable is explained by the model.

3. Is the model performing well?

Yes. If MAE is low and R² is close to 1, the model performs well.
